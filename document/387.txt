Cyber Security is the practice of protecting networks, systems, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money from users, or interrupting normal business processes. Cyber Security threats come in many forms, some of the most common being malware, phishing, and ransomware. Malware is malicious software that is designed to gain access to a user's system and cause damage. Phishing is when a malicious actor sends a message with the intent of obtaining sensitive information from the user. Ransomware is malicious software that is used to lock a user out of their system or encrypt their data until a ransom is paid.

The best way to protect yourself from cyber security threats is to be aware of the different types of threats that exist and to take measures to protect yourself against them. It is important to use strong passwords and two-factor authentication when available. Additionally, it is important to keep your operating system and other software up to date with the latest security patches. Finally, it is important to be aware of suspicious emails and websites and not to click on any links or open attachments from unknown senders.
