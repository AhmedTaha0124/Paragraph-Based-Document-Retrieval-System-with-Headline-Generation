Cyber security is the practice of protecting networks, devices, programs, and data from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money from users, or interrupting normal business processes. Cyber security is a growing field due to the increasing reliance on computer systems, networks, and the internet. To protect against cyber security threats, organizations and individuals must have a comprehensive security strategy in place to protect their data from cyber criminals. 

When it comes to cyber security, there are a variety of different types of security measures. These include antivirus software, firewalls, encryption, authentication, authorization, and other security protocols. Each of these measures can be used in combination to provide a comprehensive security plan. Additionally, organizations and individuals must stay up to date with the latest cyber security trends and developments in order to ensure they are protected from potential threats.
